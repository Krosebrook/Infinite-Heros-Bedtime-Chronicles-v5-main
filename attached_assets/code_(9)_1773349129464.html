The Whisper of the Azure Grove

The air in the Azure Grove was thick with the scent of stardust and ancient cedar. Leo stepped cautiously over a moss-covered root that pulsed with a faint, rhythmic indigo light. Every time his boot touched the ground, small motes of light danced upward like startled fireflies.

"We aren't supposed to be here after the moon peaks," whispered Mochi, his robotic companion, whose sensors were whirring in rhythmic clicks. "The elders say the trees start to dream, and their dreams can be... heavy."

Leo ignored the warning. He could see it now—the Star-Core, resting atop the Crystal Spire just beyond the clearing. It flickered like a dying candle, gasping for the energy it had lost during the Great Eclipse.

Suddenly, three paths revealed themselves through the dense fog. One path led toward the singing stream, another toward the shadowed hollow, and the third directly up the jagged rocks of the Spire.
