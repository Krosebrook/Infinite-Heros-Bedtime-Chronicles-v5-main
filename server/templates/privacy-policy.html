Privacy Policy

Infinity Heroes: Bedtime Chronicles — Last updated: June 2026

Summary for Parents: A parent or guardian must give consent before any story is created. Story data stays on your device. To generate a story, the hero details and any name/age you choose to provide are sent to our AI partners — they are not stored on our servers.

1. Information We Collect

Information Stored on Your Device (Never Sent to Us):

  • Child profiles (name, age, favorite hero) — stored locally via AsyncStorage
  • Generated stories, illustrations, and audio — cached on device
  • Badges, reading streaks, and achievements
  • App settings and parent controls (including PIN)

Information Sent to Our Server (Not Stored):

  • Hero name, title, and power (required for story generation)
  • Story preferences: mode, duration, tone, setting, soundscape
  • Child's first name (optional — used to personalize the story)
  • Child's age (optional — used to adjust story complexity)
  • Voice Chat only: the audio your child records (sent to our AI partner to generate a spoken reply)

This information is used only to generate the story or Voice Chat reply and is not stored on our servers after the response is sent.

Technical Data (Automatically Collected):

  • IP address — used for rate limiting, automatically purged after 60 seconds
  • Request IDs — random identifiers for debugging, not linked to users

2. How We Use Information

  • Generate personalized bedtime stories using AI
  • Create story illustrations and narration audio
  • Prevent abuse through rate limiting

We do not use any information for advertising, profiling, or analytics.

3. Third-Party Services

To generate stories and audio, we use the following AI services:

  • Google Gemini — text and image generation (Privacy Policy)
  • OpenAI — text and image generation (Privacy Policy)
  • Anthropic Claude — text generation (Privacy Policy)
  • ElevenLabs — text-to-speech narration (Privacy Policy)
  • Supabase Authentication — secure session management, no personal data collected (Privacy Policy)

Only the story generation inputs listed in Section 1 are shared with these services. No persistent identifiers are sent.

4. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

  • We require a parent or guardian to give consent before any story is created or information is collected
  • Any child first name or age you provide is used only to generate that story or Voice Chat reply and is not stored on our servers
  • Child profiles are otherwise stored only on the parent's device
  • We do not require account creation, email addresses, or passwords
  • We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics SDKs
  • We do not display advertisements
  • We do not enable social features, chat with strangers, or public profiles
  • All story content is filtered through child safety rules to ensure age-appropriate content

5. Data Retention

  • Device data: Stored until the user deletes the app or clears app data
  • Server-side: No user data is retained. Rate limit entries expire after 60 seconds. TTS audio cache files expire after 24 hours.
  • AI provider logs: Subject to each provider's retention policy (see links above)

6. Parental Rights

Parents and guardians can:

  • View all data stored by the app (it's all on-device in the child's profile)
  • Delete any child profile, story, or badge from within the app
  • Delete all app data by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling
  • Set parent controls (story length limits, bedtime scheduling, theme filtering) protected by PIN
  • Contact us to request information about data practices

7. Data Security

We protect data in transit using HTTPS/TLS encryption. Server endpoints are protected by rate limiting, input validation, and authentication. All AI prompts include mandatory child safety filters.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
